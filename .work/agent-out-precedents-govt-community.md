## Cedar LNG — 30+ confirmed facts
- FID June 25 2024 (signed Crystal Smith / Scott Burrows in Kitamaat Village)
- World's first Indigenous-majority-owned LNG facility
- Haisla 50.1% / Pembina 49.9%; nameplate 3.3 mtpa (up from 3.0)
- Capital: US$3.4B Class III gross; US$2.3B (~70%) fixed-price lump-sum for FLNG; US$1.1B onshore. Fully loaded with IDC ≈ US$4.0B
- 20-yr ARC Resources take-or-pay 1.5 mtpa at fixed toll; ~200 mmcf/d from Montney via Coastal GasLink
- Pembina holds mirror agreement for remaining 1.5 mtpa, intends to assign to third-party offtaker
- EPC: Samsung Heavy Industries (shipbuilder, Korea) + Black & Veatch (LNG tech)
- Project debt 60% / equity 40%; MUFG project-finance advisor; CIBC for Haisla equity raise
- Federal $200M Strategic Innovation Fund + FNFA capital; CER aggregates ~$400M federal capital
- BC $200M provincial contribution agreement for electrification
- Powered by BC Hydro renewables (lowest carbon intensity LNG claim)
- Approvals: BC EAC March 2023, federal Decision Statement 2023, Cedar LNG Pipeline permit, BCER LNG Facility Permit
- Oct 2024 amendment applications: alternate transmission re-route, distribution power line, larger marine terminal (spread mooring)
- Construction commenced early July 2024; peak onshore 2026; FLNG arrives Korea→site 2028; ops late 2028
- Pembina forward EBITDA guidance US$200-260M/yr net (50% share)
- Counsel: Baker Botts (commercial) + King & Spalding (EPC)

## OCAP + Data Sovereignty
- 4 principles: Ownership, Control, Access, Possession (FNIGC definition)
- AANDC/ISC holds 210+ data banks of FN data
- ATIP recognizes as "governments" only modern-treaty/SGA FNs + BC Participating FNs under BC Education Jurisdiction Act
- AB and NS are the exceptional provinces recognizing FN govts as "governments"
- Health Canada released de-identified NIHB data to Brogan Inc (acquired by IMS Health) for global pharma marketing
- Mohawk Council of Akwesasne — only FN holding its own NIHB+FNHIS data; enabled by enacting own FN privacy law
